Why I developed the BASA MAPUA?

BASA MAPUA, the student publication website, was born out of a vision to empower students by providing a centralized platform for staying informed and expressing themselves. As a student, I realized the need for a space where we could easily access institutional updates, stay in the loop about upcoming school events, and hone our journalism and writing skills. BASA MAPUA not only serves as an information hub but also as a creative outlet, allowing students to showcase their original writing and share their thoughts on a safe freedom wall. The word censoring feature ensures a respectful and inclusive environment for all users. This project was developed with a deep commitment to fostering a sense of community, encouraging critical thinking, and promoting transparency within our educational institution.
